When should I RSVP by?


Please RSVP by July 22nd, 2020.


What changes did you make to the schedule after postponing the wedding?


Given the wedding day switched to a Friday, the welcome events (the Callejoneada and the Welcome Cocktails) that previously took place on Friday are now after-party events. Excited to keep celebrating with you on Saturday!

Can I bring a date?


If your invitation says “and Guest,” then yes, if not, we would prefer if it was just you.

When should I arrive to San Miguel?


We suggest you arrive either Thursday night or Friday morning in order to make it to the 5:30 PM ceremony on Friday.

What time should I arrive at the ceremony?


The ceremony begins at 5PM, but we suggest you arrive 10-15 minutes earlier to guarantee seating.

Is there transportation to and from the hotels to the ceremony?


We will only be providing transportation from the ceremony to the reception, but the ceremony is close to the center of town. We recommend you bring comfortable shoes, as walking might be faster even when transportation is provided!

I have a food allergy, can I make a special request?


Please email Mariana Tellez (marianeventos@gmail.com) if you have any food allergies we should be aware of.

What should I wear?


Black tie; tuxes and long gowns.
