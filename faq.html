Keeping our friends and family safe is our priority, so we had to postpone our wedding plans twice! Do I need to wear a mask?


SMA policy requires masks for the Callejoneada and the ceremony. Please bring your masks!


Do I need a COVID test to attend wedding events?


If you are not fully vaccinated, please take a COVID test within 3 days of attending the wedding for the safety of all of our loved ones. We love you all and want to minimize the risk of anyone getting sick.


Where do I get a COVID test for return to the US?


If your trip is less than three days, you can test in the US within the three day period of your return flight. If you don’t have time or your trip is longer than three days, you can set up an appointment through your hotel or with Biotec Lab’s home-testing service for ~$35 USD. You can call 415-152-3344 or Whatsapp 415-103-2144 and someone from Biotec will do a 1-hr rapid test at your home. If you have any issues setting up the appointment please let us know – we know this is important and are happy to help!

Can I bring a date?

When should I arrive to San Miguel?


We suggest you arrive either Thursday night or Friday by 4PM to comfortably make it to the callejoneada.

What time should I arrive at the ceremony?


The ceremony begins at 5:30 PM, but we suggest you arrive 10-15 minutes earlier to guarantee seating.

Is there transportation to and from the hotels to the ceremony?


We will only be providing transportation from the ceremony to the reception, but the ceremony is close to the center of town. We recommend you bring comfortable shoes, as walking might be faster even when transportation is provided!
