Flights



Queretaro (QRO)

QRO is the closest airport to San Miguel de Allende and is a good option if San Miguel is your final (and only) destination.


Travel Time to San Miguel: 1 hr 20 min

Getting to San Miguel:
  • Shuttle Van with BajioGo ($25/person)
  • Uber (~$50)
  • Taxi (only cash or AMEX!) – There is a taxi stand to your left as you exit the airport. ~$50 dollars for a car (max 4 people) ~70 dollars for a van (max 9 people)

León/Guanajuato (BJX)

This is a good option if San Miguel is your only destination and flights to Queretaro don´t work with your schedule.


Travel Time to San Miguel: 1 hr 50 min

Getting to San Miguel:
  • Shuttle Van with BajioGo ($30/person)
  • Uber (~$55)

Mexico City (MEX)

Good idea if you would like to spend a few days before/after the wedding exploring the capital (highly recommend!).


Travel Time to San Miguel: 3 hr 30 min

Getting to San Miguel:
  • Bus departing from Terminal Central de Autobuses del Norte (~15 min. from airport). Other luxury bus companies include Primera Plus and ETN.
  • Shuttle Van with BajioGo
  • Uber (~$200)
